# V1 Evaluation Summary — Roam Landing Page Copy

**Date:** February 2026  
**Audience:** 20 personas (4 per bucket × 5 buckets)  
**Referrer:** Mix (word of mouth, Instagram/TikTok ad, search)  
**Baseline → V1 comparison**

---

## Top-Line Metrics

| Metric | Baseline | V1 | Delta |
|--------|----------|-----|-------|
| Resonance (agree+) | 15% | 50% | **+35pp** |
| Intent (agree+) | 10% | 40% | **+30pp** |
| Conversion confidence (agree+) | 10% | 35% | **+25pp** |
| Dealbreaker rate | 45% | 30% | **-15pp** |
| Clarity: nailed_it | 10% | 55% | **+45pp** |

V1 strongly outperforms the generic baseline. The "saves graveyard" headline and problem framing resonate. Conversion is below the 50% target; quiet_explorer and routine_breaker show low intent and conversion — copy isn’t yet speaking to them.

## Strongest Lines (by frequency)

1. **"Your saved reels don't have to die in a graveyard."** — saves_graveyard, overwhelmed_planner  
2. **"From saves to shortlist."** — cross-bucket  
3. **"Your weekend shouldn't take longer to plan than to enjoy."** — CTA section

---

## Most Common Objections

1. **"How it works unclear" / "Want more transparency"** — quiet_explorer, some saves_graveyard  
2. **"No group planning yet"** — group_coordinator  
3. **"Need to see the app first"** — general  
4. **"Could be more specific about how the shortlist is built"** — what_feels_off theme

---

## Recommendations for V2

- Add one clear sentence on **how** the shortlist is built (your inputs + time + preferences) to reduce “black box” and dealbreakers.
- Add a line for **routine_breaker**: e.g. “You don’t have a research problem — you have a ‘actually do it’ problem.”
- Acknowledge **group planning** on the roadmap so group_coordinator doesn’t feel ignored; keep focus on personal shortlist for V1.
