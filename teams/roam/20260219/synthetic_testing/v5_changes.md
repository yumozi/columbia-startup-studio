  **Why:** Reinforces that others are joining; reduces "am I the only one?" for quiet_explorer and routine_breaker.

- **Section:** No structural changes  
  **Change:** Kept hero, problem, how it works, objections, and dual CTA as in V4.  
  **Why:** V4 hit >50% conversion and 15% dealbreakers; V5 is final polish only.
