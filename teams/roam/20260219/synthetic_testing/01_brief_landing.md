# Synthetic User Testing Brief — Roam Landing Page

**Team:** Roam  
**Test name:** landing  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Purpose:** Test landing page copy for Roam — an app that helps time-constrained, disorganized people decide, commit, and execute on activities they actually want to do (rescue from "saves graveyard," taste-powered shortlist).

---

## 1. What We're Testing

- **Copy:** Landing page messaging (hero, value prop, how it works, social proof, objection handling, CTA).
- **Location:** Draft copy in `v1_copy.md` through `v5_copy.md` (iterative versions).
- **Goal:** See which framing, headlines, and objection handlers resonate with each persona and drive conversion intent.

---

## 2. Who's Seeing This

- **Referrer:** Mix — friend shared link, Instagram/TikTok ad, organic search ("app to actually do saved stuff").
- **Context:** They have a problem (saved reels they never do, planning friction, anxiety about time). They're evaluating whether Roam is worth trying.

---

## 3. Persona Buckets (5)

| Bucket | Description | Voice quote | Prevalence |
|--------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **saves_graveyard** | Has 100+ saved reels/posts; rarely does any. Wants to "actually do" things but feels guilty and scattered. | "I have a whole folder of places I want to try. I've been to maybe two." | 20% |
| **overwhelmed_planner** | Time-anxious (students, busy professionals). Hard to know if it's "okay" to have fun; needs time estimates and clarity. | "I never know how long things take. I'm scared to commit to a plan and then run out of time." | 20% |
| **group_coordinator** | Tired of back-and-forth and being the one who chases people. Wants commitment and visibility, not just "interested." | "I'm done being the project manager for my friend group's social life." | 20% |
| **routine_breaker** | Stuck in routine; wants novelty but doesn't actively research. Needs a nudge and shortlist, not another feed. | "I want to do something new, I just don't want to spend an hour figuring out what." | 20% |
| **quiet_explorer** | Values personal utility over social features. Skeptical of algorithms and buzzwords; wants transparency and control. | "I don't want another app that feels like a social network. I want something that actually helps me." | 20% |

---

## 4. Product Description (for clarity scoring)

**Canonical:** Roam helps time-constrained, disorganized people quickly decide, commit, and execute on activities they actually want to do — by turning saved inspiration into a taste-powered shortlist with time estimates and less friction.

**Core claim for "nailed_it":** An app that turns your saved ideas (reels, posts) into a manageable shortlist so you can actually do them, with time expectations and less overwhelm.

---

## 5. Pricing

- Free tier / waitlist for MVP. No paid tiers in copy yet; price perception can be "fair" or "good_deal" for free.

---

## 6. Copy Skeleton

- Hero (headline, subheadline, CTA)
- Problem (saves graveyard, anxiety, coordination)
- Solution / How it works (shortlist, time estimates, taste-powered)
- Social proof (interview quotes, numbers)
- Objection handling (already have apps, trust, another app, etc.)
- CTA (join waitlist / get shortlist)

---

## 7. Evaluation Criteria

Standard schema: resonance, clarity_response, intent, conversion_confidence, price_perception, strongest_line, what_feels_off, objections, dealbreaker, dealbreaker_reason. Optional: gut_reaction, unanswered_questions.

- **Resonance:** Does this speak to my situation?
- **Intent:** Would I try this?
- **Conversion confidence:** Would I sign up / join waitlist after reading?
- **Clarity:** One-sentence description of what the product does (scored separately: wrong / partial / nailed_it).

---

## 8. Stopping Criteria

- Minimum 5 rounds (V1–V5).
- Stop early if: conversion agree+ >50%, or two consecutive rounds with no meaningful change, or user satisfied.
- Audience: 20 personas (4 per bucket) for this run to allow 5 full rounds with manageable evaluation load.
