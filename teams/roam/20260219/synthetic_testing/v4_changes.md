# Changes from V3 to V4

- **Section:** Objection handling (new short objection)  
  **Change:** Added: "We're not selling your data — we use it only to build your shortlist. No ads, no social graph, no resale."  
  **Why:** quiet_explorer and privacy-conscious personas still had some hesitation. Explicit “no resale” reduces trust friction.

- **Section:** Hero  
  **Change:** Subheadline now ends with: "— and we'll give you 3–5 things to do this weekend, not 200 options."  
  **Why:** Keep “3–5 things this weekend” in the hero so routine_breaker and others see the concrete outcome immediately.

- **Section:** CTA  
  **Change:** Primary CTA option: "Get 3 ideas for this weekend →" (alongside "Join the waitlist →") so people who want the concrete outcome can click that.  
  **Why:** V3 recommendation to test CTA; “Get 3 ideas” speaks to outcome, “Join waitlist” to early access.
