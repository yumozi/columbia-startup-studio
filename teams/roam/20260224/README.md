## Roam — What it is

Roam helps time-constrained, disorganized people turn saved ideas (reels, posts, notes) into a shortlist they can actually do — with clear time estimates, less overwhelm, and no mystery algorithm. From "saves graveyard" to "3–5 things this weekend."

## Synthetic Testing Summary

We ran synthetic user testing with 20 personas across 5 buckets (saves_graveyard, overwhelmed_planner, group_coordinator, routine_breaker, quiet_explorer). Full 5-round run is in `20260219/synthetic_testing/`; here we include **V1 and V2** as evidence of at least 2 rounds.

**Results (V1 → V2):**

| Metric | V1 | V2 | Delta |
|--------|-----|-----|-------|
| Resonance (agree+) | 50% | 55% | +5pp |
| Intent (agree+) | 40% | 45% | +5pp |
| Conversion (agree+) | 35% | 42% | +7pp |
| Dealbreaker rate | 30% | 25% | -5pp |

**Key changes V1 → V2:** Added transparency line ("your inputs and our structure"), "actually do it" problem framing for routine_breaker, and group roadmap acknowledgment for group_coordinator.
