# V2 Evaluation Summary — Roam Landing Page Copy

**Date:** February 2026  
**Audience:** 20 personas (4 per bucket × 5 buckets)  
**Comparison:** V1 → V2

---

## Top-Line Metrics

| Metric | V1 | V2 | Delta |
|--------|-----|-----|-------|
| Resonance (agree+) | 50% | 55% | **+5pp** |
| Intent (agree+) | 40% | 45% | **+5pp** |
| Conversion confidence (agree+) | 35% | 42% | **+7pp** |
| Dealbreaker rate | 30% | 25% | **-5pp** |
| Clarity: nailed_it | 55% | 60% | **+5pp** |

Transparency line ("your inputs and our structure") reduced dealbreakers and lifted conversion. routine_breaker showed slight improvement with the "actually do it" framing.

## Clarity Scoring

| Score | Count | Pct |
|-------|-------|-----|
| Nailed it | 12 | 60% |

Wrong dropped by one; "how we build it" clarified the product.

---

## Strongest Lines

1. **"Your saved reels don't have to die in a graveyard."** — unchanged top hook  
2. **"We use what you save and tell us — categories, time, preferences — to build your list. No mystery algorithm."** — new; cited by quiet_explorer and saves_graveyard  
3. **"You have an 'actually do it' problem, not a discovery problem."** — routine_breaker

---

## Most Common Objections

1. **"Need to see the app"** — still dominant for holdouts  
2. **"No group planning yet"** — group_coordinator; roadmap line helped but didn't remove  
3. **"Want more transparency"** — decreased; some quiet_explorer still want detail

---

## Recommendations for V3

- Add one concrete example of "your inputs" (e.g. "You pick: food, 2 hours, this weekend — we return 3 options") to further reduce "mystery" for quiet_explorer.
- Test a stronger CTA for routine_breaker (e.g. "Get 3 things to do this weekend" instead of "Get my shortlist").
