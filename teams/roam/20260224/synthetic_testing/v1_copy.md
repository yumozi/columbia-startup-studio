## SECTION 5: OBJECTION HANDLING

### "I already have Notes / TikTok saves."
So do we. The problem isn't saving — it's that nothing turns those saves into "here are 3 things you can do this weekend." Roam does that. One place, one shortlist.

### "I don't want another productivity app."
Roam isn't about tasks or efficiency. It's about turning the stuff you *want* to do into something you actually do. No guilt, no to-do list — just a shortlist that matches your vibe and your time.

### "How do you know what I'll like?"
You tell us. Pick categories, set your time and preferences. The more you use it, the better the shortlist gets. No social graph, no influencer algorithm — just your taste.

### "What about planning with friends?"
We're starting with your personal shortlist and time estimates. Making commitment and coordination easier is on the roadmap. For now, we're focused on helping you decide and commit for yourself.

### "I don't want another app sending me notifications."
You're in control. Set when you want a shortlist (e.g., Thursday for the weekend) or just open the app when you're ready. No engagement farming.
