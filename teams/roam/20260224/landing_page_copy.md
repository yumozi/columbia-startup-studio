## How It Works

**Section headline:** From saves to shortlist.

**Step 1 — Bring your inspiration in:** Save links or pull from your saves. Roam keeps your ideas in one place — no more digging through five apps.

**Step 2 — Set your constraints:** When are you free? How much time do you have? We show you time estimates so you know what's actually doable.

**Step 3 — Get a shortlist, not a feed:** We surface a small list of things that match your taste and your schedule. Not 200 results. A shortlist you can choose from and actually do.

**How we build it:** We use what you save and tell us — categories, time, preferences — to build your list. No mystery algorithm, just your inputs and our structure. **Example: you pick food, 2 hours, this weekend — we return 3 options from your saves and our catalog. You choose; we don't push.**

## Objection Handling

**"I already have Notes / TikTok saves."**  
So do we. The problem isn't saving — it's that nothing turns those saves into "here are 3 things you can do this weekend." Roam does that. One place, one shortlist.

**"I don't want another productivity app."**  
Roam isn't about tasks or efficiency. It's about turning the stuff you *want* to do into something you actually do. No guilt, no to-do list — just a shortlist that matches your vibe and your time.

**"How do you know what I'll like?"**  
You tell us. Pick categories, set your time and preferences. The more you use it, the better the shortlist gets. No social graph, no influencer algorithm — just your taste. **Example: tag 'food' + 'Saturday afternoon' + 'under 2 hours' → we surface a shortlist from what you've saved and what fits.**

**"What about my data?"**  
**We're not selling your data — we use it only to build your shortlist. No ads, no social graph, no resale.**

**"What about planning with friends?"**  
We're starting with your personal shortlist and time estimates. Making commitment and coordination easier is on the roadmap — we heard you. For now, we're focused on helping you decide and commit for yourself.

**"I don't want another app sending me notifications."**  
You're in control. Set when you want a shortlist (e.g., Thursday for the weekend) or just open the app when you're ready. No engagement farming.
