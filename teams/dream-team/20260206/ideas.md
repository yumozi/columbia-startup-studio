## Notes & Observations

- Recruiting workflows are fragmented across tools, links, and intuition.
- Coaches value **time savings**, **consistency**, and **trustworthy signal extraction**.
- Structured frameworks (film tagging, fit scoring) reduce cognitive load and disagreement.
- Add-on modules may lower adoption friction versus all-in-one replacement platforms.
